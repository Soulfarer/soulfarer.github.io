About

Skyfarer is a mixed-reality single-player game designed specifically for rehabilitation centers to serve wheelchair-bound patients who suffer from spinal-cord injuries and require routine exercises for health and strengthening. Soulfarer provides an immersive, interactive and engaging experience for patients to exercise, grow, and track improvements all while having fun. This helps therapists monitor growth and allows gamers to enjoy happier and healthier lives. Soulfarer is a join Advanced Games Project between USC Games and USC Creative Media and Behavioral Health Center
